iAlonso Printer

About

This is my first 3D printer, called iAlonso Printr. It uses 3mm PLA plastic, it has 5 motors controlled by a computer; it can print 200mm × 180mm × 180mm objects (7.87 × 7.08 × 7.08 inches). The video below shows the first object that was printed.
